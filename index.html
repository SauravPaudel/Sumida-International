About Us

Welcome to Sumida Educational International Consultancy

Embark on a transformative educational journey with Sumida Educational International Consultancy, your trusted partner for abroad study counseling with a specific focus on Japan. At Sumida, we understand that pursuing higher education in a foreign country is a significant decision, and we are committed to providing you with comprehensive support and guidance throughout the entire process. Our team of experienced and knowledgeable consultants is dedicated to assisting ambitious students like you in achieving their academic goals in Japan.
